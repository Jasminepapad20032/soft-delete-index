Soft Delete Index

Fragments of websites that don't know they are already gone.

I’m treating this page like an abandoned directory page. The links look alive, but most of what they point to is already drifting, pixelated, or half missing. Soft delete: the server forgot to tell the memories they’re gone.

  • rotting-memories.html

    saved screenshots, missing contexts, ghosts of profiles.

  • error_poems.css

    404 messages that talk back instead of apologising.

  • gallery.php

    looping glitches, abandoned banners, loading forever.

  • link-rot-index.php

    this one used to lead somewhere but it forgot where.

This site is intentionally messy and incomplete. The internet forgets quietly; this is me trying to notice it. Each page is a small artefact of digital decay – an index of things half-erased, not quite gone, still flickering underneath the noise.
